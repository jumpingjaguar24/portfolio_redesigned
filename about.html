Hi, I'm Joe.

I'm a Brooklyn-based full-stack engineer and UI/UX designer with a passion for building innovative, accessible, and user-centered solutions.

I’m looking for opportunities where I can connect with people and create real-world solutions that make a meaningful difference in their lives.

When I’m not working from a coffee shop or relaxing at home, you can usually find me experimenting in the kitchen, running through the streets of NYC, or hanging out in the park with friends.
